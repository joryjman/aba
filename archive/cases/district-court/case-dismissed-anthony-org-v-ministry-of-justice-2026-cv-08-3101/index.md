Anthony_org

v.

Ministry of Justice

This civil action arises from the unlawful arrest, detention, and incarceration of Plaintiff Anthony_org by employees of the Ministry of Justice (“MoJ”) without due process of law. Plaintiff was jailed without a hearing, without an opportunity to defend themselves, and under a presumption of guilt rather than innocence.

These actions violated the Constitution of Azalea Isles, specifically:

Article 1, Section 9 – Right to self-representation or counsel in a court of law
Plaintiff brings this action to protect constitutional rights and to prevent continued abuse of executive authority by the Ministry of Justice.

PARTIES

Plaintiff:
Anthony_org
Defendant:
The Ministry of Justice
STATEMENT OF FACTS

On 1/12/2026, Plaintiff was arrested by employees of the Ministry of Justice.
Plaintiff was immediately jailed without:
a judicial hearing,
a formal charge being adjudicated by a court,
or an opportunity to defend themselves.
No court order authorizing incarceration was issued prior to Plaintiff being jailed.
Plaintiff was treated as guilty upon arrest.
Plaintiff was not afforded the presumption of innocence.
No opportunity was provided to present evidence or challenge the allegations prior to punishment.
After Plaintiff contested the charges the arresting officer responded "I don't give a singular fuck"
LEGAL CLAIMS

Plaintiff was denied the opportunity to represent themselves or obtain counsel before incarceration.
Punishment was imposed prior to any judicial process.
This conduct violates Article 1, Section 9 of the Constitution of Azalea Isles.
DAMAGES

As a direct result of Defendant’s actions, Plaintiff suffered:
unlawful loss of liberty,
emotional distress,
reputational harm,
deprivation of constitutional rights.
$350 azalean dollars
Plaintiff respectfully requests that this Court:

Issue a judicial finding that the Ministry of Justice violated:
Article 1, Section 9 of the Constitution of Azalea Isles;
Declare Plaintiff’s arrest and incarceration unconstitutional;
Award compensatory damages in an amount determined by the Court;
Award punitive damages in the amount of $10,000;
Award compensatory damages in the amount of $350;
Award legal damages equal to 20% of damages awarded;
Grant injunctive relief preventing MoJ personnel from:
incarcerating citizens without judicial adjudication,
presuming guilt prior to trial;
Grant any further relief the Court deems just and proper.
I, Anthony_org, hereby affirm that the allegations in this complaint AND all subsequent statements made in court are true and correct to the best of my knowledge, information, and belief, and that any falsehoods may bring the penalty of perjury.
