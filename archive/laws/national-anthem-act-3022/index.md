## Full Text

Original Legislation:
Passed Bill Link: https://www.cityrp.org/threads/national-anthem-act.2934/
Final Vote: 8-0-0
Ratified: December 5, 2025 (via referendum)

Author: Hanuta Brehmer, MP
Sponsor: Hanuta Brehmer, MP

Economic Integrity Act
Preamble: In times of division and constant disputes among the Azalean people, a symbol of unity may bring back Azalean spirit, securing an unified appearence not only internally but also externally. The establishment of an anthem shall symbolise our union, justice and internationally diverse people.

1. Text and Music
(a) The song 'Freedom Azalea!' by Hanuta 'RealHanuta' Brehmer shall become the offical anthem of the Azalean Isles.
(b) There shall be no official version of the text, which may appear in any language present within the people. Yet, the English version shall be proclaimed as the main one.

2. Usage of the Anthem
(a) The instrumental and text of the anthem are public domain; the composition of the score may be altered by any person and translations can be done as required. There is no obligation to credit the composer or lyricist.

Enactment: This bill shall be voted upon by the people in a national referendum not segregated by electoral district, and will go into effect if approved by a majority of voters.
