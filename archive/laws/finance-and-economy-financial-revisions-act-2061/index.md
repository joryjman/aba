Amendments:
Amended July 28, 2025 by the Amendment to the Financial Revisions Act

Author: Lysander Lyon, MP
Sponsor: Lysander Lyon, MP

Financial Revisions Act
Preamble: With the pending repeal of the Miscellaneous Changes & Foreign Affairs Omnibus Act, this bill reestablishes the business limits while also changing the tax on bank revenues.

1. Short Title
(a) This bill shall be referred to as the "Financial Revisions Act".
(b) This bill was authored by Lysander Lyon.

2. Business Limits
(a) There shall be no limit on the number of private enterprises a player may own, but each private enterprise must serve a clear and legitimate purpose.
(b) Private enterprises may be forcibly closed by the Ministry of Economic Affairs if they do not serve a clear and legitimate purpose in the eyes of the Ministry.
(i) This does not mean the Ministry can close businesses it seems redundant, but it may close in-game private enterprises if they do not seem to actually be active, and are instead acting as shell companies.

3. Commercial Bank Taxes
(a) The Financial Institution Establishment Act, section 2(a)i, shall be amended to the following: 25% of monthly profits must be paid to the Ministry of Economic Affairs.

Enactment: This Act comes into force immediately upon passage.
