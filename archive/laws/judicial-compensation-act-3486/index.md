Judicial Compensation Act
Preamble: Judicial officers should receive a salary.

1. Short Title & Enactment
(a) This bill shall be referred to as the “Judicial Compensation Act”.
(b) This bill was authored by Lysander Lyon.

2. District Court Judges
(a) District Court Judges shall receive a salary of $20 per 20 minutes.

3. Supreme Court Justices
(a) Supreme Court Justices shall receive a salary of $30 per 20 minutes.

Enactment: This Act comes into force immediately upon passage.
