Mailbox Act
Preamble:
This bill aims to allow the addition of mailboxes to the sidewalk area in front of a plot.

1. Short Title and Enactment
(a) This bill shall be referred to as the "Mailbox Act".
(b) This bill was authored by Nanicholls MP.
(c) This bill shall be enacted immediately.

2. Definition of a Mailbox
(a) A 1x1 chest, barrel or mailbox, 1 block in front of a plot, that serves the purpose of receiving deliveries from a mail company or delivery service.

3. Implementation
(a) MUD will be expected to allow players or businesses to apply for installation of a mailbox.
(b) MUD will charge a $50 installation fee per mailbox.
(c) The player or business will be expected to provide the required block(s).

Enactment: This Act comes into force immediately upon passage.
