Mines Transportation Act
Preamble: It is the obligation of the government and legislative to ensure a safe environment for allcitizens of Azalea, including the ones that are willing to put themselves into serious danger to provide for the greater cause. Everyone of its visitors has experienced, that traversing the mines is a tedious and dangerous endeavour. This bill seeks to establish a transportation system for the mines to make travelling through Azaleas underground tunnels more safe and time efficient.

1. Establishment of the Transportation System
(a) The Azalean mines shall hereby be equipped with a transportation system. The official name for said transportation system shall be Mines Transportation System (hereafter also referred to as Transportation System).
(b) The Transportation System must be connected to every currently available and newly available level of the mines. Its planning, construction and administration shall be taken over by the Ministry of Urban Development in accordance with the Governing Structure Act section 6.

2. Purpose of the Transportation System
(a) The Transportation System shall not serve the purpose of public transit. Only miners shall be able to take advantage.
(b) The Transportation System shall give its users the possibility to reach every desired and through
their skills available mine in an instant. The number of reachable mines is subject to individual progress in the quest system of the miner.

3. Further Regulations
(a) Regular routes leading through the mines shall not be affected by the establishment of the Transportation System.

Enactment: This Act comes into effect immediately upon passage of this bill.
