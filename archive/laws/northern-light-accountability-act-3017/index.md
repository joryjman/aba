Northern Light Accountability Act
Preamble: The government makes money from our taxes, we should know how much of it is in its accounts and how much each ministry asks for in their budgets each month. This will be known as the “Northern Light Accountability Act”.

1. General (Untitled)
(a) Each month, in the first week of the month, the Prime Minister must publish what the government has earned in taxes from citizens and other government revenue streams.
(b) Each month the budget for the ministries and Bank Of Azalea must be posted publicly for the citizens to view within the first week of the month.
(i) This must be posted on the discord under a new channel called “monthly-budget” that is viewable by citizens.
(c) Each posting must be clearly detailed this includes:
(i) A screen shot of the full government balance when the Prime Minister pulls up the balance in game.
(ii) Detailed reports from the ministries on what each budget is to be used for if it is increasing by more than 50%.
(iii) Detailed reports to why a ministry is requesting less funding if their budget drops by more than 50%.
(iv) Detailed reports to why a ministry had to request additional funds from the government if they must request more funds in the monthly budget period.
(d) If the Prime Minister fails to release the report on time, they will be fined a total of $7,000.
(i) For each additional day that the posting is late, the Prime Minister will be fined an additional $1000 until the report is completed and posted.

Enactment: This Act comes into force immediately after passage.
