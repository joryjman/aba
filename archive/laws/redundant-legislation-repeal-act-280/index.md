Author: Jebediah Crumplesnatch MP
Sponsor: N/A
Type: Act of Parliament

A
BILL
TO
Reduce Red-Tape and Repeal Redundant or Unenforced Legislation

Preamble: Bureaucracy is a useful tool when applied correctly. This bill aims to remove laws where it was not.

1. Archiving Repealed Legislation
(a) A new section will be created on the Forum for Repealed laws
(i) This section will be titled "Repealed Laws"
(ii) The Speaker will be empowered to move Acts of Parliament to this section.

2. Improper Parking Act
(a) The Improper Parking act will be repealed.

3. Colonial Flag Act
(a) The Colonial Flag act will be repealed.

(a) The Election Procedure act will be repealed.
