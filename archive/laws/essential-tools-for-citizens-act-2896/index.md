Essential Tools for Citizens Act
Preamble: This act ensures that no new player is disadvantaged in their ability to gather resources within the isles to make their game experience easier, participate more in the economy, or contribute to the development by having easier access to the mines or to the wild for the wood.

1. Short Title
(a) This bill shall be referred to as the "Essential Tools for Citizens Act".

2. Basic Tools Guarantee
(a) Provision of Tools
(i) The Government shall ensure that every new citizen of the nation is provided with:

(ii) One (1) Iron Pickaxe

(iii) One (1) Iron Axe

Enactment: This Act shall come into force 1 week upon passage
